Gameduino: $53¶

Gameduino is the Arduino shield that turns your Arduino into a gaming system.

This is the assembled and tested Gameduino; plug it into your Arduino and immerse yourself in 8-bit awesomeness.

The Gameduino handles scrolling backgrounds, 256 sprites, and 64-voice stereo sound. While the Gameduino is handling the video and audio, the Arduino runs the controllers and gameplay.

The GD library includes dozens of clear code samples and demos that show all its capabilities.

Know the whole machine. The Gameduino quick reference poster gives you instant understanding of the graphics system.
