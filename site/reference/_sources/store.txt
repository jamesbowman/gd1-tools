Gameduino is the Arduino shield that turns your Arduino into a
gaming system.

This is the assembled and tested Gameduino; plug it into your Arduino
and immerse yourself in 8-bit awesomeness.

The Gameduino handles scrolling backgrounds, 256 sprites, and
64-voice stereo sound.  While the Gameduino is handling the video
and audio, the Arduino runs the controllers and gameplay.

The GD library includes dozens of clear code
:ref:`samples and demos <samples>`
that show all its capabilities.
